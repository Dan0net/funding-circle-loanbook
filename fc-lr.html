We are extending the types of loan we offer

This forms part of our wider commitment to help thousands of small businesses access the finance they need to grow.

We have been working with many small businesses who are property developers and investors since we launched in 2010 and now we have tailored our loans to allow us to help even more businesses like them.

All of these loans will be bid on a fixed rate, and will be funded on a first come, first served basis.

Small business property development loans

For small businesses who develop property.

Interest is paid to investors monthly. All of the principal is due to be repaid at the end of the loan term.

Small business property investment loans

For small businesses looking to buy their own premises or buy and lease properties to others.

Interest and some principal is repaid monthly, with the remaining principal due at the end of the loan term. Typically loans will have a 1 - 5 year term, with an amortisation profile up to 25 years.

If you want to read up more on property loans, go to our FAQs.
